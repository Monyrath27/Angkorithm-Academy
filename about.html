About Angkorithm Academy

Hello! I am Prak Keomonyrath, a Cambodian Computer Science student and the founder of Angkorithm Academy. I started this platform with a vision to empower students worldwide, especially young women and underrepresented learners, to excel in STEM and technology.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student has equal access to high-quality education in technology and STEM, and the opportunity to become innovators, problem-solvers, and leaders in their communities.


Our Mission

  • Provide affordable, high-quality, and practical courses in IT and STEM fields, including Web Development, AI, Mobile Apps, and Data Science.
  • Offer both free and paid courses to ensure accessibility for all learners.
  • Equip students with real-world skills, hands-on projects, and certificates to enhance career opportunities.
  • Inspire confidence, creativity, and leadership among students globally.
  • Support Cambodian students while building a platform that connects learners worldwide.

What We Offer

At Angkorithm Academy, students can explore:

  • Interactive online courses designed by industry professionals.
  • Practical projects that simulate real-world challenges.
  • Guidance, mentorship, and community support for learners at every level.
  • Certificates of completion to showcase skills to employers and universities.

Why Choose Angkorithm Academy?

We combine accessibility, quality, and innovation to ensure that students worldwide—regardless of background—can unlock their potential and thrive in technology careers. Our platform is not just about learning; it’s about empowering the next generation of global tech leaders.

Join us at Angkorithm Academy and start building your future in STEM today.
